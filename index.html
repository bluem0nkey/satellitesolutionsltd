Digital Aerials & Satellite

Digital aerials and satellites can be cost effective way to optimize your entertainment yet still have it free of monthly subscription. If you prefer listening to the radio than watching TV, you can have FM and DAB aerial which will give you access to a vast range of stations. If you prefer both watching TV and listening to Music than you can always have a combination aerial.

Integrated Reception System

We, at RJSNA specialise in IRS, which means you can get an entertainment package even though you live in a multi dwelling building. We can provide individual packages in a flat within a block a flats. With property developments we can ensure your new build is aerial and satellite entertainment friendly and your tenants or buyers are not limited when it comes to entertainment package.

Multi-Room Viewing

You can watch TV in any room at any time, you can start a film downstairs and finish off in the bedroom! (SKY Q) You can watch the same program on all the TVs or watch different programs. A multiroom system can be completely customized and tailored to suit your viewing requirements. A multiroom system can be fully configured with any digital home entertainment system, allowing each room in your property to receive and enjoy any digital aerial or satellite TV system you currently have installed.

Wall Mounting

We have professionals that can mount/remove your TV (plasma, LED or LCD) of any size on the wall safely and securely. The wires and cables are neatly and discreetly secured. We ensure that our installations are as sleek and faultless as your entertainment system.

Sky Dish

If you need new sky dish or need a repair or need to upgrade the existing one, we can help in a most cost effect way at your convenience.

Free Sat

If freeview reception is weak, we can use Freesat. Freesat is also free of monthly subscriptions and has a great range of digital UK satellite channels, including mainstream entertainment, news and radio listings, it provides over 150 TV and radio channels including HD channels.
